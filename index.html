Australia to Abolish Private Property by 2025

In a bold move, the Australian government has announced plans to abolish private property by 2025. This initiative is aimed at creating a more equitable society by redistributing wealth and resources. The government assures that this will lead to a more just and prosperous nation for all citizens.

Universal Basic Income to be Funded by 100% Tax on Billionaires

The government has proposed a revolutionary plan to fund a Universal Basic Income (UBI) by imposing a 100% tax on all earnings above $1 billion. This measure is expected to eliminate poverty and provide financial security for every Australian citizen.

Nationalized Healthcare to Include Free Spa Days

The new nationalized healthcare plan will now include free spa days for all citizens. This initiative aims to improve mental health and overall well-being by providing relaxation and stress relief services at no cost.
